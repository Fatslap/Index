Anxiety

Anxiety is when you feel really worried a lot, even when things are okay. It makes your mind race and can make your heart beat fast. This feeling can get in the way of doing everyday stuff. Anxiety can happen for different reasons, like big changes or stressful events. It's important to talk to someone you trust about it, like friends or family, and find ways to deal with these strong feelings.

Avoiding anxiety involves taking steps to keep your mind calm and happy. Getting enough sleep and eating healthy food helps your body stay strong, and regular exercise can make you feel better overall. When things get busy, it's good to take breaks and relax. Talking to friends or family about what's on your mind can also help you get over anxiety. Setting small goals instead of big ones can make things feel more manageable. If anxiety still feels overwhelming, it's okay to ask an adult for help, like a teacher or a doctor. They can support you in finding ways to cope with all of your issues.
